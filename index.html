Bolani is one of Afghanistan's traditional famouse food. Bolani is a delicious dish similar to a dumpling, that is, a dough similar to bread dough is used, and vegetables such as leeks and chives are used in the dough. Some people add some potato to the ingredients inside the bolani, which is completely tasteful. Then they fry in hot oil and enjoy!
